Five-Way Strengths

C

Chase Ultimate Rewards

Best for Hyatt and United MileagePlus instant transfers. Simple 1:1 across all 14 partners with no devaluations.

C

Citi ThankYou

Only major program with American Airlines AAdvantage 1:1 transfers. Also strong for Qatar, Turkish, and LifeMiles.

C1

Capital One Miles

Best breadth: many 1:1 instant partners and unique access to Air Canada Aeroplan, British Airways, and Turkish Miles&Smiles at full 1:1.

B

Bilt Rewards

The only program to earn points on rent with strong partners like World of Hyatt. Includes Alaska, JAL, and United.

A

Amex Membership Rewards

Unmatched airline coverage including exclusive partners like ANA and Delta. Look out for frequent transfer bonuses.

Data sources: The Points Guy transfer partner guides for Chase, Citi, Capital One, Bilt, and Amex (updated Feb 13). All ratios verified. Transfer times: Chase — instant except Singapore KrisFlyer (~48h). Citi — instant to 14 days. Capital One — mostly instant; Cathay Pacific 24h, EVA Air 36h. Amex — mostly instant; ANA ~48h. US airlines incur an excise fee on Amex transfers.

Transfer Notes

  • • Chase: 1,000-point increments, instant except Singapore ~48h
  • • Citi: 1,000-point minimum, instant to 14 days (typically <24h)
  • • Capital One: 1,000-mile minimum, mostly instant (Cathay 24h, EVA 36h)
  • • Bilt: 2,000-point minimum for most transfers (1,000 for elite members), mostly instant
  • • Amex: 1,000-point increments, mostly instant. Excise tax offset fee applies to US airlines
  • • Always confirm award availability before transferring — transfers are irreversible
