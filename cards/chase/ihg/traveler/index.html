The "Premier Downgrade"

Rather than burning a Chase 5/24 slot directly on a no-annual-fee card, points experts recommend this path:

  1. 1.Apply for the IHG Premier Card ($99 AF) when it has a massive bonus (e.g., 140k-185k points or 4-5 Free Nights).
  2. 2.Enjoy the Premier perks for Year 1 (Platinum status, Anniversary Free Night).
  3. 3.After 12 months, call Chase and downgrade the card to the no-annual-fee Traveler card.
