Our Recommendation

Choose Qatar if...

You want the SFO-DOH flight for a Maldives/Bali honeymoon for lower miles and double bed now. At 70k off-peak, you'll save 85k miles for two vs Singapore – that's a night at Alila Villas.

Choose Singapore if...

You're prioritizing food and the direct SFO-SIN nonstop (17h15m, no connection), and willing to pay more miles. Book the Cook + no Doha transit is serene.
